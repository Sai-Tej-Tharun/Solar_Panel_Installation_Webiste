Our Story

Built on a Simple
Belief in the Sun

Solar-Echos was born in a small office in Pune in 2013, when our founders — two electrical engineers and a sustainability consultant — noticed something troubling: India had boundless sunlight but millions of homes still relied on expensive, polluting grid power.

We started with just five team members and a mission to make solar accessible for every Indian household and business. Our first installation was a 4 kW rooftop system for a local school in Nashik. Watching those students learn under clean power for the first time lit a spark that has never gone out.

Today, Solar-Echos operates across 18 states, employs over 480 certified professionals, and has installed more than 12,400 solar systems — from individual homes to 5 MW commercial farms.
