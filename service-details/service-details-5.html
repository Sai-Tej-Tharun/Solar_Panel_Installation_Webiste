Service Overview

A solar system without regular maintenance is like a car without service — it gradually underperforms without you noticing. Our AMC plans keep your system generating at its peak rated output for the full 25-year system life.

Dust, bird droppings, pollen, and soiling can reduce solar panel output by 10–25% if left uncleaned. Loose DC connections, degraded bypass diodes, and inverter faults silently drain your system's efficiency. Our quarterly maintenance visits catch and resolve all these issues before they become expensive problems.

Our AMC plans cover residential systems from 1 kW to 100 kW and commercial/industrial systems from 100 kW upward. We service all brands — Solar-Echos installations as well as third-party systems from any installer.

What Every Maintenance Visit Includes

1

Visual & Structural Inspection

We inspect all panels for micro-cracks, delamination, discolouration, hotspots (using thermal imaging on Premium plans), and bird damage. Mounting structures are checked for rust, loose bolts, and structural fatigue. Cables are inspected for UV degradation and rodent damage.

30–45 minutes
2

Professional Panel Cleaning

Panels are cleaned with deionised (DI) water and soft brushes — leaving zero residue and no scratches. High-pressure water is never used (it can damage panel edges and seals). Bird dropping stains are treated with a mild alkaline solution.

45–90 minutes depending on system size
3

Electrical Testing

We measure open-circuit voltage and short-circuit current for each string, check all DC isolator switches and MC4 connectors for heat damage, test the RCCB and AC isolator, verify inverter input voltage/current readings against expected values, and check for any insulation resistance faults.

30–60 minutes
4

Inverter Service & Firmware

We check inverter cooling fans and vents for dust blockage, clear error codes, update firmware if a new version is available, verify grid-protection relay settings, and compare live power output against the irradiance reading to calculate performance ratio.

20–30 minutes
5

Earthing & Safety Check

Earthing continuity and earth resistance are measured and compared to MNRE/IEC standards. Lightning protection components are visually inspected. Surge protection devices (SPDs) are tested and replaced if degraded.

15–20 minutes
6

Service Report & Recommendations

You receive a digital report within 24 hours with before/after cleaning photos, test readings, any issues found (with photo evidence), actions taken, parts replaced, and recommendations for the next maintenance window.

Sent within 24 hours
