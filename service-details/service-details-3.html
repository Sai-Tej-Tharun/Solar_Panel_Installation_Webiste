Service Overview

Solar-Echos supplies and installs lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO₄) battery storage systems that let you store surplus daytime solar energy for use at night, during power cuts, or during expensive peak-tariff hours.

LiFePO₄ technology is the gold standard for home and commercial energy storage — safer, longer-lasting, and more efficient than lead-acid alternatives. With 6,000+ charge-discharge cycles, a 10 kWh battery bank will last 16+ years under daily cycling, and comes with our 10-year performance warranty.

Our battery systems integrate seamlessly with any solar installation — new or existing — through a hybrid inverter that intelligently manages solar, battery, and grid sources. The Solar-Echos monitoring app gives you live visibility into battery state of charge, charge/discharge rates, and projected backup duration.

LiFePO₄ vs Lead-Acid: Why it matters

LiFePO₄ batteries last 4–5× longer, charge 2× faster, allow 100% depth of discharge, require zero maintenance, and contain no toxic lead or acid — making them the only sensible choice for a long-term solar investment.

Installation Process

1

Load Assessment & Sizing

We analyse your critical loads (refrigerator, fans, lights, medical equipment) and typical solar surplus hours to recommend the correct battery capacity. Oversizing wastes money; undersizing causes dissatisfaction — we get it right the first time.

Day 1 — Included in survey
2

Hybrid Inverter Selection

For new solar installations, we size and select a hybrid inverter that manages solar, battery, and grid inputs simultaneously. For retrofit projects on existing solar, we audit the current inverter for battery compatibility and replace if necessary.

Day 2–3
3

Battery Mounting & Wiring

Battery modules are wall-mounted or floor-mounted in a well-ventilated location (utility room, garage, or outdoor weatherproof enclosure). DC cabling from the battery to the hybrid inverter is installed with proper fusing and disconnect switches.

4–8 hours on-site
4

BMS Configuration & Testing

We configure the Battery Management System parameters — charge voltage limits, discharge cut-off, temperature thresholds, and grid interaction rules. The system is tested through a full charge-discharge cycle before handover.

Same day
5

App Setup & Handover

Your Solar-Echos monitoring app is configured to show live battery state of charge, estimated backup time, charge/discharge history, and total energy stored. You receive full warranty documentation and a user manual.

Day of installation

LiFePO₄ (lithium iron phosphate) is the safest lithium chemistry — it does not catch fire or undergo thermal runaway under normal conditions. It also offers 6,000+ cycles vs 500–800 for lead-acid, 100% usable capacity vs ~50% for lead-acid, and zero maintenance. Compared to NMC (the chemistry in most EV batteries), LiFePO₄ runs cooler, lasts longer, and is inherently more stable.
