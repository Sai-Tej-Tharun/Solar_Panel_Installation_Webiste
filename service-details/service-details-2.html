Service Overview

Solar-Echos designs, supplies, and commissions commercial-scale solar PV systems for industries, institutions, and large commercial establishments seeking to dramatically cut electricity costs and meet sustainability mandates.

Our commercial solar division specialises in rooftop installations on industrial sheds, warehouses, factory buildings, and office complexes; as well as ground-mounted systems on unused land. We manage the complete project lifecycle — feasibility study, shadow analysis, structural assessment, DPR preparation, DISCOM/CERC approvals, EPC execution, and O&M.

A 100 kW rooftop system on a factory in Maharashtra generates approximately 12,000–14,000 kWh per month, replacing grid power at ₹8–11 per unit — delivering annual savings of ₹11–15 lakh. With CAPEX financing and accelerated depreciation benefits (40% in Year 1 under IT Act), the effective payback period is typically 3–4 years.

40% accelerated depreciation available

Under Section 32 of the Income Tax Act, commercial solar assets qualify for 40% accelerated depreciation in Year 1, significantly improving post-tax ROI for companies.

Our Commercial Installation Process

1

Energy Audit & Feasibility Study

We analyse 12 months of electricity bills, demand profile, load patterns, and roof/ground area to model the optimal system size, layout, and financial projections including NPV, IRR, and payback period.

Week 1 — Free of charge
2

Detailed Project Report (DPR)

We prepare a comprehensive DPR covering system design, single-line diagram, structural load calculations, shadow analysis, equipment specifications, and a month-by-month yield estimate. The DPR is required for bank financing and DISCOM approval.

Week 1–2
3

Regulatory Approvals & Financing

Our regulatory team handles DISCOM synchronisation approval, CERC/SERC grid connectivity norms, net metering or group captive registration, and assists with loan documentation for SBI/IREDA/bank financing at competitive rates.

Week 2–5
4

Procurement & Civil Work

Tier-1 commercial panels (Waaree, Longi, Trina), string or central inverters, industrial-grade mounting structures, AC/DC cabling, MV transformer (if needed), and SCADA equipment are procured under strict QA. Civil work for mounting and cabling runs concurrently.

Week 3–6
5

EPC Installation & Commissioning

Our EPC crew installs the complete system — structures, panels, inverters, HT/LT cabling, earthing, lightning protection, and SCADA. On commissioning day, we run full load tests, verify protection relay settings, and sync with the DISCOM metering point.

Week 5–9
6

Handover & O&M Onboarding

You receive complete as-built drawings, test certificates, warranty documents, SCADA login, and a training session for your in-house team. An O&M contract is offered to keep the system at optimal performance for the next 25 years.

Week 9–10 — Live & generating

Frequently Asked Questions

We handle commercial projects starting from 10 kW (small offices and clinics) upward. For our Industrial package, the typical starting point is 100 kW. There is no upper limit — we have delivered projects up to 5 MW. Any size above 10 kW qualifies for our commercial pricing and commercial-grade equipment.
Yes. Under open access regulations, consumers with a contracted demand above 1 MW can source solar power from a third-party generator located elsewhere. We assist with open access application, cross-subsidy surcharge calculation, and long-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) structuring with solar IPPs.
Under Section 32 of the Income Tax Act, solar power generating sets are classified as renewable energy devices and qualify for 40% depreciation in Year 1 (written-down value method). For a ₹50L system in the 25% tax bracket, the Year-1 tax saving is approximately ₹5L, effectively reducing the net system cost by 10%.
Yes. Under our OPEX model (also called a PPA or RESCO model), Solar-Echos funds, installs, and maintains the system at no upfront cost to you. You pay only for the solar units consumed at a pre-agreed rate (typically 20–30% below grid tariff) for a 15–25 year term. This is ideal for companies that prefer to conserve capital.
We offer lightweight mounting solutions for pre-engineered building (PEB) steel sheds and older RCC structures. If structural reinforcement is needed, our civil team handles it. Alternatively, carport structures in parking areas or ground-mounted systems on unused land are excellent options that avoid roof load concerns entirely.
