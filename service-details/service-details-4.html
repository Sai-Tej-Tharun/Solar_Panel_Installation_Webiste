Service Overview

Solar-Echos designs and installs integrated EV charging solutions for homes, housing societies, offices, and commercial charging stations — all optimised to run on solar power for maximum savings and minimum carbon footprint.

Charging an EV from the grid costs approximately ₹8–10/unit. Charging from rooftop solar costs effectively ₹0 for daytime charging, and as little as ₹1.5/unit when using stored solar energy at night. A typical EV owner driving 40 km/day saves ₹1,800–₹2,200 per month compared to grid charging, and ₹3,500–₹4,500 compared to petrol.

We install Type-2 AC chargers (7.4 kW single-phase, 22 kW three-phase) for home and office use, CCS2 and CHAdeMO DC fast chargers for commercial applications, and smart chargers compatible with Tata Nexon EV, MG ZS, Hyundai Ioniq, Ather, Ola Electric, and all major EV brands available in India.

Installation Process

1

Site Survey & Load Assessment

We assess your parking area, existing electrical panel capacity, distance from panel to parking, and your EV's onboard charger rating. We check if your single-phase or three-phase supply can support the charger without panel upgrades.

Day 1 — Free
2

Charger Selection & Solar Integration Design

We recommend the right charger (7.4 kW AC for home, 22 kW AC or 50 kW DC for commercial) and design the solar integration circuit. If you have an existing solar system, we check hybrid inverter compatibility for solar-priority charging mode.

Day 2–3
3

Electrical Panel Upgrade (if needed)

If your MCB/ELCB panel requires an additional circuit or load balancer, our electricians upgrade it. A dedicated 32A circuit is wired from the panel to the charger location, with appropriate cable sizing for minimum voltage drop.

Day 3–4 (if required)
4

Charger Installation & Commissioning

The wall-mount or pedestal charger is installed at the parking spot with weatherproof cable management. We test full-speed charging with your actual EV, verify solar-priority mode operation, and confirm all safety protections (RCCB, surge protection).

3–5 hours on-site
5

App Setup & Handover

Your Solar-Echos app is configured with the charger's live status, energy consumed per session, and monthly cost savings. You receive a 3-year charger hardware warranty, CE certification, and user manual.

Same day — You're charging!

In solar-priority mode, the smart charger communicates with your hybrid inverter via RS485 or Wi-Fi. When surplus solar power exceeds a set threshold (e.g., 3 kW), the charger automatically starts charging your EV with that free excess solar energy. If solar production drops, the charger either throttles down to match available solar or switches to grid — depending on your preference settings.
