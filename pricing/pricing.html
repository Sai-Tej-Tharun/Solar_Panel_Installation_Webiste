Yes — every price shown on this page is fully inclusive of 12% GST, all equipment, professional installation by our certified technicians, and necessary commissioning. There are no add-on surprise charges. The only thing not included is optional battery storage on Basic and Standard plans, which is shown separately as an add-on.

Solar-Echos manages the entire PM Surya Ghar subsidy application on your behalf — from document collection to portal submission. The subsidy (up to ₹78,000 for 3 kW residential systems) is a direct bank transfer from the government to your account, typically within 30–60 days of installation completion. You do not pay the full amount upfront; we structure the payment schedule to account for the incoming subsidy.

We provide a written Generation Guarantee on all Standard and Premium plans. If your system generates less than the projected output (verified monthly via our monitoring platform), Solar-Echos will service your system at no charge and compensate for any shortfall in generation greater than 10%. Our average system performs within 5% of projections across all Indian climates.

Yes — all our plans are designed with future expansion in mind. You can add panels (up to your sanctioned load), upgrade to a hybrid inverter, or add battery storage at any point. Upgrade pricing is applied on the delta cost only, not the full system. Upgrades are available at a 10% loyalty discount for existing Solar-Echos customers.

Our typical timeline from confirmed booking to live generation is 7–14 working days for residential systems. This includes site survey (Day 1–2), permit application and approval (Day 3–7), and physical installation (1–2 days). Commercial systems above 50 kW typically require 3–6 weeks due to structural and electrical approval processes. We provide a live project tracker via the homeowner dashboard throughout.

Solar panels are rated to produce power for 25–30 years, degrading at roughly 0.5% per year. After warranty expiry, Solar-Echos offers affordable Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) starting at ₹3,999/year for continued monitoring, cleaning, and preventive maintenance. Our field service network covers 18 states, so you are never without support.
